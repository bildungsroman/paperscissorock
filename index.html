Who We Are

Kevin Hudson, founder & designer

Kevin holds a BA in multimedia communication design from the School of Art and Design at Texas State University, one of the top design programs in the U.S. In the rare moments when he's not behind the computer with Photoshop and Illustrator open, he enjoys playing music and learning about animation. He's also quite handy with a hammer or saw.

Anna Spysz, writer & developer

Anna earned a BA in English (with a very useful minor in Astronomy) at the University of Texas in Austin, after which she spent over a decade abroad, earning a master's, running a newspaper, writing several books, and consulting for European startups. These days she's learning to code python and working on web development projects. You can see more of her writing on her personal portfolio page.
